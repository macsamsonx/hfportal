Data Privacy Statement

Data Privacy Notice — Hundredfold Digital Marketing

Pursuant to Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Hundredfold Digital Marketing informs you of the following before collecting your personal data:

1. Data Controller

Hundredfold Digital Marketing is the Personal Information Controller responsible for your personal data collected through this form.

2. Personal Data Collected

We collect your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, home address, and any other information you voluntarily provide in this registration form.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is collected and processed for the following purposes: (a) evaluating your employment application; (b) creating and managing your employee account upon approval; (c) administering employment-related activities including payroll, attendance tracking, and performance management; and (d) compliance with applicable labor laws.

4. Legal Basis

Processing is necessary for the performance of, or in preparation for, an employment contract to which you are a party, and to comply with legal obligations applicable to the employer.

5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained for the duration of your employment and for a period required by applicable Philippine labor and tax laws after the termination of employment (typically 10 years). Applications that are not approved will be retained for 1 year and then deleted.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be sold or disclosed to unauthorized third parties. Data may be shared with government agencies (SSS, PhilHealth, PAG-IBIG, Bureau of Internal Revenue) as required by Philippine law, and with authorized HR personnel for employment administration purposes only.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under RA 10173, you have the following rights: (a) right to be informed; (b) right of access to your personal data; (c) right to correction of inaccurate data; (d) right to erasure or blocking, subject to applicable legal retention requirements; (e) right to object to processing; (f) right to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

8. Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related concerns or to exercise your data subject rights, contact our HR Manager at the office or through the official company email.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or destruction.
