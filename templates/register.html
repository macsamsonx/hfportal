Data Privacy Statement

Data Privacy Notice — Hundredfold Digital Marketing

Pursuant to Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Hundredfold Digital Marketing informs you of the following before collecting your personal data:

1. Data Controller

Hundredfold Digital Marketing is the Personal Information Controller (PIC) responsible for your personal data collected through this form.

2. Personal Data Collected

We collect your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, home address, and skills/position information you voluntarily provide in this registration form.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is collected and processed for the following purposes: (a) evaluating your employment application; (b) creating and managing your employee account upon approval; (c) administering employment-related activities including payroll, attendance tracking, and performance management; and (d) compliance with applicable labor laws and regulations of the Philippines.

4. Legal Basis

Processing is based on: (a) your consent given through this form; (b) the necessity of processing for the performance of, or in preparation for, an employment contract to which you are a party; and (c) compliance with legal obligations applicable to the employer under Philippine law including the Labor Code of the Philippines, DOLE regulations, SSS Act, PhilHealth Act, Home Development Mutual Fund Act, and the National Internal Revenue Code.

5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained for the duration of your employment and for a period required by applicable Philippine labor and tax laws after the termination of employment (typically not less than 10 years, per DOLE and BIR requirements). Applications that are not approved will be retained for 1 year and then securely deleted.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be sold or disclosed to unauthorized third parties. Data may be shared with: (a) government agencies including the Social Security System (SSS), Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth), Home Development Mutual Fund (Pag-IBIG), and Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) as required by Philippine law; (b) authorized HR personnel and management for employment administration purposes only.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), you have the following rights: (a) right to be informed; (b) right of access to your personal data; (c) right to rectification of inaccurate data; (d) right to erasure or blocking, subject to applicable legal retention requirements; (e) right to object to processing; (f) right to data portability; (g) right to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at complaints@privacy.gov.ph or through www.privacy.gov.ph.

8. Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related concerns or to exercise your data subject rights, contact our HR Manager or Data Protection Officer at the office or through the official company email. Hundredfold Digital Marketing is committed to designating a Data Protection Officer as required under NPC Circular 2023-04.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures consistent with the requirements of the National Privacy Commission to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

10. Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting our HR Manager in writing. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal, and does not affect processing based on other legal bases.
