Data Privacy Policy

Hundredfold Digital Marketing · Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Hundredfold Digital Marketing ("the Company") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and the issuances of the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

2. Data Controller

The Company acts as the Personal Information Controller (PIC). Our Data Protection Officer may be contacted through the HR Manager.

3. Personal Data Collected

  • Identity: Full name, gender, date of birth
  • Contact: Email, phone, home address
  • Employment: Position, skills, department, shift, employment type
  • Financial: Hourly rate, payroll, government ID numbers (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, TIN)
  • Attendance: Clock-in/out, leave and overtime records
  • Emergency contact: Name and contact number

4. Legal Bases & Purposes

  • Consent (Sec. 12[a]): Processing data you voluntarily provide
  • Contractual necessity (Sec. 12[b]): Payroll, attendance, leave administration
  • Legal obligation (Sec. 12[c]): SSS Act (RA 11199), PhilHealth Act (RA 7875), HDMF Law (RA 9679), NIRC, Labor Code (PD 442)
  • Legitimate interests (Sec. 12[f]): Performance management and internal operations

5. Data Retention

Employment records: minimum 10 years (BIR/DOLE). Unprocessed applications: 1 year then deleted. Attendance/leave: minimum 3 years.

6. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with: government agencies (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, BIR, DOLE) as required by law; authorized HR/Admin personnel; service providers under data processing agreements.

7. Your Rights (Data Privacy Act Sec. 16–18)

You have the right to: be informed, access your data, rectify inaccurate data, erasure/blocking (subject to retention laws), object to processing, data portability, and file a complaint with the NPC.

To exercise rights: contact our HR Manager. Response within 15 working days.

To file a complaint: National Privacy Commission · complaints@privacy.gov.ph · www.privacy.gov.ph

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (password hashing, role-based access, on-premise storage) consistent with NPC requirements.

9. Data Breach

We will notify the NPC and affected data subjects within 72 hours of a breach, per NPC Circular 16-03.

10. Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw consent in writing at any time. Withdrawal does not affect lawfulness of prior processing or processing based on other legal bases.
